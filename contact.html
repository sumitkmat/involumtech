FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and support.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical MVP takes 8-12 weeks, while enterprise solutions may take 4-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase and keep you updated with bi-weekly sprint reviews.

What is your development methodology?

We follow Agile/Scrum methodology with 2-week sprints, daily standups, and regular client demos. This ensures transparency and allows for iterative improvements. We use tools like Jira, Slack, and GitHub for seamless collaboration.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive post-launch support packages including bug fixes, performance monitoring, feature updates, and 24/7 emergency support. We also provide SLA-based maintenance contracts for enterprise clients.

What technologies do you specialize in?

Our core expertise includes React, Angular, Node.js, Python, AWS, Azure, Docker, Kubernetes, and AI/ML frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and scalability needs.

How do we get started?

Simply fill out the contact form above or email us at hello@involume.tech. We'll schedule a free consultation call to understand your requirements and provide a detailed proposal within 48 hours. No commitment required!
